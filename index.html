About Brady

Brady Hakes is a student at Utah Valley University studying communications and sociology. He is currently employed at UVU as a peer mentor for the Melisa Nellesen center. Throughout his career Brady has worked with a variety of people from diverse backgrounds and has learned how to accommodate many different types of needs and communication styles. After graduating in early 2024 Brady plans to pursue a career in instructional design and encourage the development of more third places.

Background

Neurodiversity

Because most of his siblings are Neurodiverse Brady has been involved in the disability community for most of his life. During his employment at scenic view and UVU Brady has worked alongside and on the behalf of a number of Neurodiverse individuals. From these experiences Brady has fostered familiarity and connections with the Neurodiverse community.

Transit familarity

Brady first started riding the bus regularly shortly after attending classes at UVU. Since then Brady has utilized transit to get to a variety of destinations throughout Utah county. He has even helped a friend learn how to get to and from work with a nearby bus line.
